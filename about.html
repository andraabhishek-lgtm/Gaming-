Our Story

We Are The Leading
Racing Esports Platform

Founded in 2016, Gaming Stackly has grown from a small racing community into one of the world's premier esports platforms. Our mission is to give racers — from sim enthusiasts to professional circuit drivers — the ultimate competitive stage.

We believe in the power of speed to unite people, create unforgettable moments, and push the limits of competitive motorsport entertainment.

What Drives Us

Mission & Vision

Every decision we make is guided by our commitment to racing excellence and community growth.

🏁 Our Mission

  • Connect racing game players globally through competitive tournaments
  • Provide equal opportunity for grassroots and professional racers
  • Deliver world-class event production and broadcast coverage
  • Support drivers with coaching, analytics, and career development tools
  • Bridge the gap between virtual racing and real-world motorsport

👁 Our Vision

  • Become the world's #1 racing esports organization by 2028
  • Launch a certified racing esports league in every major continent
  • Partner with Formula 1, MotoGP, and WRC for official esports series
  • Develop next-gen sim racing hardware and platform technology
  • Create pathways for esports racers to enter real motorsport careers
