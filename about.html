Our Story

We Are Building a
More Compassionate World

Founded in 2014, HopeRise Foundation started with a single community kitchen in Mumbai. Today, we are one of India's most trusted humanitarian organizations — running 200+ programs in 80+ countries covering education, healthcare, food relief, environmental action, and children's welfare.

We believe every person — regardless of background — deserves dignity, opportunity, and hope. With 5,000+ volunteers and 12,000+ donors, we are proof that collective compassion changes the world.

What Drives Us

Mission & Vision

Every decision we make is guided by our commitment to human dignity and lasting change.

❤ Our Mission

  • Deliver education, healthcare, food, and shelter to vulnerable communities
  • Empower women and children through rights-based programs
  • Restore ecosystems through community-led environmental action
  • Build resilient communities prepared to withstand future challenges
  • Operate with full transparency — every rupee accounted for publicly

🎯 Our Vision

  • A world where every child can access quality education by 2030
  • Zero preventable deaths from hunger or curable disease in our communities
  • Plant 10 million trees and restore 100+ degraded ecosystems by 2028
  • Expand to 100+ countries with locally-led, self-sustaining programs
  • Inspire 1 million people to volunteer at least once in their lifetime
