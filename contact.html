Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining the race. Can't find your answer? Contact us directly.

01

How do I register for a racing tournament?

Head to our Tournaments page, choose your discipline, and click "Register". Fill in your driver profile and preferred class — you're on the grid in minutes.

02

What racing disciplines are available?

We host Formula Esports, MotoGP Leagues, Touring Car Championships, Off-Road Rally, and open Sim-Racing cups across multiple platforms.

03

How are prize pools distributed?

1st Place — 50%, 2nd Place — 25%, 3rd Place — 15%. Remaining 10% is shared among other finalists. Full breakdown shown on every tournament page.

04

What equipment do I need for sim-racing?

A compatible racing wheel and pedals are recommended, but keyboard and gamepad entries are accepted in select categories for all skill levels.

05

Can brands become a racing sponsor?

Absolutely. We offer trackside branding, jersey placement, broadcast integration, and exclusive title sponsorships. Email partners@gamingstackly.com to get started.

06

How do I report a fair play violation?

Email support@gamingstackly.com with match details, timestamps, and any recordings. Our integrity team reviews all reports within 24 hours.
