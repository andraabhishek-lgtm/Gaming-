Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about donating, volunteering, or partnering with us. Can't find your answer? Reach out directly.

01

How do I make a donation to HopeRise Foundation?

Click "Donate Now" in the navigation, choose your cause area, and select your donation amount. We accept UPI, net banking, credit/debit cards, and cheques. All donations above ₹500 receive an 80G tax exemption certificate.

02

Is my donation tax-exempt under 80G?

Yes. HopeRise Foundation is registered under Section 12A and has 80G certification. You can claim up to 50% deduction on your donation. We email your official receipt within 48 hours of payment.

03

How is my donation used?

35% goes to Education & Literacy programs, 28% to Healthcare & Wellness, 22% to Food & Nutrition, and 15% to Environment & Child Welfare. We publish quarterly impact reports on our website.

04

How can I volunteer with HopeRise Foundation?

Visit our Volunteers page and click "Join a Squad". You can choose from field volunteering, digital support, or event coordination. Our team will contact you within 3 working days with onboarding details.

05

Can my company partner with HopeRise Foundation?

Absolutely. We offer CSR partnerships, employee volunteering programs, cause-linked branding, and co-branded campaigns. Email donate@hoperisefoundation.org or fill the form above and select "CSR & Corporate Partnership".

06

How do I get my 80G donation certificate?

Your tax receipt and 80G certificate are emailed automatically to your registered email within 48 hours of a successful donation. For bulk or cheque donations, contact donate@hoperisefoundation.org.
