Who We Are

A Foundation Built
on Compassion

Founded in 2014, HopeRise Foundation has grown from a small community kitchen into one of India's most trusted humanitarian organizations — running life-changing programs across education, healthcare, food relief, and environment.

We believe every person deserves dignity, opportunity, and hope. Together with our 5,000+ volunteers and 12,000+ donors, we are building a more equitable world.
