Our Promise to You

Our Commitment

HopeRise Foundation operates with full transparency, integrity, and accountability in every program we run.

Program Standards

Every program follows our impact framework — clear targets, timelines, beneficiary selection criteria, and defined success metrics reviewed quarterly.

100% Fund Utilization

At least 85% of every donation goes directly to field operations. Administrative costs are published annually in our public financial report.

Beneficiary Dignity

We work with communities, not for them. All programs are co-designed with local leaders to ensure cultural sensitivity and lasting impact.

Child Safeguarding

All field staff and volunteers undergo background checks. Our Child Safeguarding Policy is zero-tolerance and reviewed by an independent board.

Impact Reporting

Donors receive quarterly impact reports with field photos, beneficiary stories, and expenditure breakdowns — no vague promises, real evidence.

Donor Support

Dedicated donor relations team available Monday–Saturday. 80G certificates issued within 7 working days of any contribution.
