Get Started

How to Join a Race

Four simple steps from sign-up to the starting grid.

1

Create Account

Sign up and build your driver profile with your platform, region, and preferred discipline.

2

Choose Tournament

Browse active events, filter by discipline, and check registration slots before they close.

3

Register & Pay Entry

Complete your entry with team details. Some events are free-to-enter with open qualifying.

4

Race & Win

Compete through qualifying, group stages, and finals. Prize money paid within 48 hours of results.

Race Rules

Tournament Regulations

All events run under the Gaming Stackly Sporting Code — fair, transparent, competitive.

Format & Rules

All tournaments follow the Gaming Stackly Sporting Code. Bracket format varies by event — single elimination, round-robin, or points-based seasons.

Prize Distribution

Prizes paid to top finishers within 48 hours of the final. Breakdown: 1st 50%, 2nd 25%, 3rd 15%, remaining 10% split among finalists.

Driver Requirements

All drivers must be verified with a valid platform account. No active bans from any sanctioned racing league. Minimum age: 16.

Anti-Cheat Policy

All competitive sessions are monitored by our integrity team. Violations result in immediate disqualification and season-long suspension.

Live Streaming

Finals are broadcast live on Twitch and YouTube with professional commentary, lap data overlays, and replay analysis.

Race Support

Dedicated race stewards are on-call during all live events. Disputes must be filed within 15 minutes of the incident occurring.
